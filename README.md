## About Me
I am a programmer with a great passion for coding. I discovered programming at the age of 13 when I created my first
small HTML website. It was simple, but it made me want to learn more.

At 14, I started learning Python seriously. It wasn’t always easy, but I enjoyed solving problems and learning from the
challenges. That’s when I knew this was what I wanted to do.

To improve my skills, I began using PyCharm, a professional tool by JetBrains. Since then, I have worked on many
projects and learned something new every day.

While working on my Secondary School Certificate, I looked for a company to begin my internship for advanced technical
school. I found a company that was a perfect fit for my field of interest, application development, and it turned out
to be the ideal place for me to grow. After nearly three years of Python experience, I started working intensively on
web development there. I learned JavaScript and PHP while also studying Java at school.

Everything feels right, and I now feel confident about entering the software industry. Programming isn’t just a hobby
for me – it’s something I truly love.
